{{- else }}
   Port-forward OPA to test locally:
     kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "pgauthz.fullname" . }}-opa 8181:8181
{{- end }}

3. SECURITY CHECKLIST before any real use:
   - Override secrets.* (authzenDirect/pgauthzdRw passwords, opaAdminToken,
     nativeServiceToken) — the defaults are DEV ONLY. Prefer
     secrets.existingSecret + a secret store.
   - Replace opa.jwks with your IdP's JWKS (or switch OPA authn to a JWKS URL).
   - Keep opa.requireTokenForReads=true and authzen ALLOW_SUBJECT_OVERRIDE=false
     unless a trusted PEP fronts the stack.
   - Enable database.backup for the audit log (system of record).
